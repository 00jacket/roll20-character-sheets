Basic Moves:


Trying to hide a Clue from the Bad Guy? Why not try a shell game? When you try to trick a suspect, roll +Charisma. On a 10+, choose two. On a 7-9, choose one.

  • the bluff is effective
  • there are no personal repercussions for the bluff
  • no one else experiences repercussions for the bluff
  • the bluff is timely


You have a suspect right where you want ‘em. When you ask a suspect some hard questions roll +Toughness. on a 10+, choose two. On a 7-9, choose one.

  • Is the suspect telling the truth?
  • How could I get the suspect to ______?
  • What does the suspect want?
  • How could I get the suspect to do what I want?


You intimidate someone into either doing something they wouldn’t normally do, or giving you something. You may intimidate by . . .

  • Being pushy (Roll +Toughness),
  • Using sleight of hand (Roll +Agility),
  • Spouting psesudoscience (Roll +Intelligence),
  • Being so hot you’re out of their league (Roll +Charisma), or
  • Doing something bizarre (Roll +Weirdness).
10+ Choose 2 from the list.
7-9 Choose 1 from the list.
  • They don’t resent you.
  • They run away.
  • They give you what you want.
  • They don’t try to trick you.


This mystery isn’t going to solve itself. When you search for clues, roll +Intelligence.
10+ You find a clue. Tell the Animator what it means.
7-9 You find a clue. The Animator tells you what it means.
6- The Animator picks one: You find a clue but you misunderstand it. Or, you don’t find a clue.


You have enough clues to nail this bad guy. Capturing a bad guy requires 2 things: a successfully executed plan and someone to explain the mystery. Have a player make a plan. Then, when you are ready to capture the Bad Guy, roll 2d6 unmodified.
10+ You ably trap the bad guy. A player must use “It was Old Man Winters!” to end the mystery.
7-9 The bad guy is hindered, but still mobile. The players must escape a chase and try again. If the bad guy has already been hindered, they are captured.


Bad guys are scary! Why stick around and see if the bad guy wants to share your pizza? Run! Roll +Agility. 10+ You out-run your pursuer. 7-9 You out-run your friends.


Once per episode, any member of the gang may use these. Smelling salts revive a character that has passed out from Fear and remove 2 Fear points.


Sometimes you need people outside the gang to do things for you. Manipulation requires Leverage. To manipulate, roll +Charisma.
10+ Choose 2 from the list.
7-9 Choose 1 from the list.

  • They act immediately.
  • They don’t need something in exchange.
  • They do it well.
  • They are happy with the bargain.

If there is no Smart One in the game, add this to the list of Basic Moves:

Once the bad guy is captured, its time to solve the mystery! Roll +Intelligence.
10+ You correctly explain all of the items on the list.
7-9 Pick two of the items on the list. You are wrong about the other one.

  • Who did it.
  • Why they did it.
  • Where the treasure is (if there is treasure).
